Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our cybersecurity services and solutions.

How quickly can you respond to a security incident?

+

Our incident response team provides a guaranteed response time of under 15 minutes for retainer clients. For non-retainer emergency incidents, we typically respond within 1-2 hours. Our 24/7 SOC monitoring clients receive immediate automated alerts and human analyst review within minutes of threat detection.

What's the difference between penetration testing and vulnerability assessment?

+

Vulnerability assessments use automated scanning tools to identify known security weaknesses in your systems and provide a comprehensive list of vulnerabilities. Penetration testing goes deeper - our ethical hackers actively attempt to exploit vulnerabilities to gain unauthorized access, simulating real-world attacks. We recommend quarterly vulnerability assessments with annual penetration testing for comprehensive coverage.

Do you offer month-to-month contracts or require long-term commitments?

+

We offer flexible options. Most of our managed services (SOC, vulnerability management) are available on month-to-month contracts with no long-term commitment required. However, we provide significant discounts (10-20%) for annual commitments. Project-based services like penetration testing and architecture design are one-time engagements with optional installment payment plans.

What industries do you specialize in?

+

We serve clients across multiple industries including financial services, healthcare, e-commerce, technology startups, manufacturing, and professional services. Our team has deep expertise in industry-specific compliance requirements like PCI-DSS for payment processors, HIPAA for healthcare, POPIA for South African businesses, and GDPR for companies with EU customers.

Can you help us achieve ISO 27001 certification?

+

Yes, our Compliance & Governance service is specifically designed to help organizations achieve ISO 27001, PCI-DSS, SOC 2, and other certifications. We conduct gap analyses, implement required controls, develop documentation, and prepare you for third-party audits. The process typically takes 6-12 months depending on your current security posture.

What makes your security awareness training effective?

+

Our training combines interactive microlearning modules with real-world phishing simulations. Rather than boring annual training sessions, employees receive engaging 5-10 minute lessons monthly, followed by simulated phishing attacks to test retention. We track metrics like click rates and reporting speed, and provide personalized feedback. Gamification and leaderboards drive engagement and friendly competition.

Is the Startup Security Essentials package suitable for established businesses?

+

The Startup Security Essentials package is optimized for companies with 5-50 employees and limited IT infrastructure. If you're an established business with more complex needs, we recommend starting with a security posture assessment to determine the right mix of services. Many of our enterprise clients started with the startup package and scaled up as they grew.

Do you provide cyber insurance support?

+

Yes, we regularly assist clients with cyber insurance applications and renewals. Many insurers now require security assessments, penetration testing reports, and proof of security controls before underwriting policies. Our documentation meets insurer requirements and we can work directly with your insurance provider to demonstrate your security posture.

What happens if we experience a breach while under your protection?

+

Our SOC and managed services are designed to prevent, detect, and respond to threats in real-time. In the unlikely event of a successful breach, our incident response team immediately activates to contain the threat, minimize damage, and restore operations. For managed SOC clients, this response is included at no additional cost. We also provide detailed forensic analysis and work with you to prevent recurrence.

Can you integrate with our existing security tools?

+

Absolutely. Our platform integrates with hundreds of security tools including firewalls, endpoint protection, cloud security platforms, ticketing systems, and SIEM solutions. We believe in enhancing your existing investments rather than requiring complete replacement. During onboarding, we audit your current tools and design an integration strategy that maximizes visibility and automation.
