Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services, pricing, and support.

What's included in the onboarding and setup process? ▼
All Fully Managed and Enterprise plans include a comprehensive onboarding process that typically takes 2-4 weeks. We start with a complete infrastructure audit and documentation of your current systems. Our team then sets up monitoring tools, implements security protocols, configures backup systems, and establishes communication channels. We also provide employee training sessions to ensure your team knows how to access support and understands any new processes. For Enterprise clients, we create detailed runbooks and disaster recovery procedures tailored to your business.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time? ▼
Yes, you have flexibility to change plans. Upgrades take effect immediately – you'll gain access to all additional features right away, and we'll prorate the billing. Downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle to ensure you receive full value for the month you've paid for. However, the Fully Managed plan does require a 6-month minimum commitment, so you can downgrade to Advisory only after completing that period. Enterprise agreements have custom terms that we'll discuss during the sales process. Simply contact your account manager or our support team to discuss plan changes.
What happens if I exceed my support hours on the Advisory plan? ▼
Advisory services are billed hourly at $150/hour with no predetermined limit. We track time in 15-minute increments and always provide estimates before starting significant work. If a project is taking longer than initially estimated, we'll notify you immediately and discuss options. We'll never charge you for time without your awareness. Most clients who consistently use more than 8-10 hours monthly find better value in our Fully Managed plan ($1,200/mo with unlimited support tickets), which we'll recommend if we notice this pattern in your usage.
Do you provide 24/7 emergency support for critical outages? ▼
Yes, for Fully Managed and Enterprise clients. We provide true 24/7/365 support with guaranteed response times for critical issues (complete system outages, security breaches, or data loss events). Fully Managed clients receive 1-hour response for critical issues, while Enterprise clients get 15-minute response times. Our on-call team can be reached by phone, email, or SMS. For Advisory clients, standard support is during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM EST, Monday-Friday), but emergency after-hours support can be arranged on a case-by-case basis at premium rates. We recommend upgrading to Fully Managed if you need guaranteed emergency support availability.
What industries and compliance requirements do you specialize in? ▼
We have deep expertise in highly regulated industries including healthcare (HIPAA compliance), financial services (PCI-DSS, SEC regulations), legal firms (attorney-client privilege protection), and professional services. Our team has successfully guided clients through HIPAA audits, GDPR implementation, SOC 2 Type II certification, and various state-specific privacy laws. For Enterprise clients, we provide hands-on compliance support including documentation, technical controls implementation, audit preparation, and ongoing monitoring. We also work with your compliance officers and legal team to ensure our technical solutions align with your regulatory obligations.
Are hardware and software licenses included in the monthly price? ▼
No, our pricing covers professional services, management, and monitoring only. Hardware purchases (servers, computers, networking equipment), software licenses (Microsoft 365, Adobe, antivirus, etc.), and cloud infrastructure costs (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) are billed separately and remain under your control. We believe in transparency – you own all your licenses and infrastructure. However, we can absolutely help you optimize these costs. Many clients save 20-30% on their software and cloud spending through our vendor relationships, license optimization reviews, and right-sizing recommendations. We provide detailed estimates before any external costs are incurred and can help you budget for technology expenses.
How quickly can you respond to support requests? ▼
Response times vary by plan and issue severity. Advisory clients receive email responses within 48 business hours. Fully Managed clients get 4-hour response for priority issues and 1-hour response for critical outages (system down, security breach). Enterprise clients receive 1-hour response for priority issues and 15-minute response for critical emergencies. All response times are measured during your service hours (business hours for Advisory, 24/7 for Fully Managed and Enterprise). We also prioritize issues by business impact – a server outage affecting your entire team gets faster attention than a single user's password reset. Resolution times depend on complexity, but we provide regular status updates throughout the process.
What's your process for handling security incidents? ▼
We follow industry-standard incident response protocols. When a security event is detected (through our 24/7 monitoring or your report), we immediately assess the severity and contain the threat to prevent spread. Our team investigates the root cause, documents all findings, and implements remediation steps. For Fully Managed and Enterprise clients, we provide detailed incident reports including timeline, impact assessment, and recommendations to prevent recurrence. Enterprise clients also receive post-incident reviews with executive summaries suitable for board presentations. We maintain an incident response playbook customized for your environment and conduct annual tabletop exercises to ensure readiness. All security incidents are handled with strict confidentiality.
Can you help us migrate from our current IT provider? ▼
Absolutely. We've successfully migrated dozens of businesses from other providers (or from self-managed IT) with zero downtime. Our migration process includes: comprehensive documentation of your current environment, coordination with your existing provider for knowledge transfer, phased migration plan to minimize disruption, parallel running period to ensure stability, and extensive testing before final cutover. We handle all the heavy lifting including extracting documentation from previous providers, reconfiguring access credentials, and validating that all systems work correctly. For Enterprise clients, we provide white-glove migration services with dedicated project management. Most migrations are completed within 30-45 days depending on environment complexity.
Do you support remote/hybrid work environments? ▼
Yes, this is one of our specialties. We design and support IT infrastructure specifically optimized for distributed teams. This includes secure VPN or zero-trust network access, cloud-based productivity tools (Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Slack), endpoint security for home/remote devices, mobile device management, video conferencing optimization (Zoom, Teams), and support for BYOD (bring your own device) policies. We help ensure your remote employees have the same security protections and reliable access as they would in an office environment. Our remote support capabilities mean we can troubleshoot and resolve issues for employees anywhere in the world without requiring physical access to devices.
