HIRING PROCESS

Your journey with
Croloft starts here

1

Find your match

As a dynamic IT startup, you will find a wide variety of positions available across our core domains that fit your expertise. Learn about the types of roles we offer by visiting our Careers section.

Apply to any positions you feel are a match with your talents. Whether you write clean JavaScript, manage cloud infrastructure, or design premium UI/UX, your application goes directly to our core engineering and creative leads, not a centralized recruiter.

2

Refine your portfolio and apply

Allow 20-30 minutes for the application process, including an updated resume and links to your GitHub, live web apps, or design portfolio referring to your specific experience for each position.

Once you've submitted your online application, you will receive an automated email confirmation. We review technical applications carefully, so if you don't hear back immediately, it is just a reflection of our thorough review timing.

3

Interviewing

It's time for us to get to know each other! While the process may differ slightly between front-end developers, IT support, and creative staff, this is a chance for you to find out more about our no-backend serverless architecture and if we will be a good match for you.

Technical Assessment: A practical coding or design challenge related to our stack (HTML, CSS, JS, API integration).

System Architecture: Discussing how to build robust, high-end applications without traditional backend engineering.

Culture & Team Fit: A conversational interview with the founders and team leads to ensure alignment with our corporate yet simple philosophy.

4

Review offer

If you've received an offer of employment, we advise you to take a moment to review our unique startup benefits, learn about our culture, and follow up with the hiring manager if you have any additional questions about the role.

If you did not receive an offer, don't give up! First, ask for constructive feedback regarding your technical assessment. Then, keep refining your skills and look out for future opportunities as we scale.
