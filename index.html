Microsoft Surface is a series of touchscreen-based personal computers, tablets, and interactive whiteboards designed and developed by Microsoft, running the Windows operating system.
Certain high-end devices in the Surface lineup, like the Surface Laptop Studio, feature dedicated NVIDIA GeForce RTX graphics cards, making them capable of handling modern gaming workloads smoothly.
It depends on your workflow. For ultimate portability and note-taking, the Surface Pro is ideal. For traditional typing with a premium feel, choose the Surface Laptop, and choose the Studio models for heavy rendering or creative design work.
Surface devices generally come pre-loaded with a 30-day trial of Microsoft 365. Full ongoing access requires an active individual, family, or commercial Microsoft 365 subscription tier.

FAQs

What is a crypto affiliate program?
A crypto affiliate program lets you earn commissions by referring new users to cryptocurrency platforms such as exchanges, wallets, and trading apps. When someone signs up or makes a transaction through your unique referral link, you receive a percentage of the fees they generate — often for the lifetime of their account. No trading experience or crypto ownership is required.
How do I get paid as an affiliate?
Affiliates are paid weekly or monthly depending on the program. Payouts are made in USDT, BTC, or direct bank transfer. A minimum payout threshold applies (typically $20–$50), and all earnings are tracked in real time through your dashboard. ChainX processes all payouts every Friday.
What commission rates can I expect?
Commission rates range from 20% to 50% of the trading fees your referrals generate. Some programs offer tiered rates — the more active referrals you bring in, the higher your percentage. Exact rates are shown per program in your dashboard.
Do I need to own crypto to become an affiliate?
No. You don't need to hold, trade, or invest in cryptocurrency to earn commissions. All you need is an audience — a blog, YouTube channel, social media, newsletter, or website — and a referral link. Your earnings are based entirely on the activity of the people you refer.
How do I track my referrals and earnings?
Every affiliate gets access to a real-time dashboard showing clicks, sign-ups, trading activity, and commission — broken down by program, date, and referral. You can also access data exports and integrate with our API if you want to pull stats into your own tools.
How do I get started?
Getting started takes less than 5 minutes. Click Join as Affiliate, fill in your details, and you'll be approved instantly. From there you can browse available programs, grab your referral links, and start sharing. Our team can also provide creatives, landing pages, and promotional materials.
