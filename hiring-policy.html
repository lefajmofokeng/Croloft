Hiring Policy - Cronos IT Solutions

Hiring Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

Cronos IT Solutions (Pty) Ltd is committed to attracting, recruiting, and retaining talented professionals who share our values and contribute to our mission of delivering exceptional IT services. This Hiring Policy outlines our recruitment principles, processes, and standards to ensure fair, transparent, and effective hiring practices across all our operations.

1. Equal Opportunity Employment

Cronos IT Solutions is an equal opportunity employer committed to building a diverse and inclusive workforce. We comply with all applicable South African employment legislation, including the Employment Equity Act, and adhere to the principles of non-discrimination.

  • All employment decisions are based on qualifications, skills, experience, and merit
  • We do not discriminate based on race, gender, age, disability, religion, sexual orientation, marital status, pregnancy, ethnic origin, or any other protected characteristic
  • We actively promote diversity and inclusion in our recruitment practices
  • We implement affirmative action measures in line with our Employment Equity Plan

2. Recruitment Process

2.1 Job Posting

  • All vacancies are advertised internally for at least 5 business days before external posting (except for entry-level positions)
  • Job descriptions clearly outline responsibilities, requirements, qualifications, and competencies
  • Postings include information about compensation, benefits, and employment terms where applicable
  • Positions are advertised on our website, job boards, professional networks, and other appropriate channels

2.2 Application Review

  • All applications are reviewed by HR and the relevant hiring manager
  • Candidates are assessed based on their qualifications, experience, and alignment with job requirements
  • Shortlisted candidates are contacted within 10 business days of the application deadline
  • All applicants receive confirmation of receipt and notification of their application status

2.3 Interview Process

  • Interviews are conducted by a panel of at least two people, including HR representation
  • Standardized interview questions ensure fair and consistent candidate evaluation
  • Technical positions may include practical assessments, coding tests, or technical interviews
  • Interviews assess both technical competencies and cultural fit
  • Candidates have the opportunity to ask questions and learn about our company culture

2.4 Background Checks and Verification

Before making a final offer, we conduct appropriate background checks, including:

  • Verification of qualifications and certifications
  • Reference checks with previous employers (minimum of two references)
  • Criminal background checks for positions involving security access or sensitive data
  • Credit checks for financial positions (with candidate consent)
  • Right-to-work verification for all employees

3. Selection Criteria

Candidates are evaluated based on the following criteria:

  • Qualifications: Relevant educational background and professional certifications
  • Experience: Demonstrated experience in relevant roles and technologies
  • Technical Skills: Proficiency in required technical competencies and tools
  • Soft Skills: Communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and adaptability
  • Cultural Fit: Alignment with our values, work ethic, and collaborative approach
  • Potential: Capacity for growth, learning, and advancement within the organization

4. Types of Employment

We offer various employment arrangements to meet business needs and provide flexibility:

  • Permanent Employment: Full-time positions with comprehensive benefits
  • Fixed-Term Contracts: Project-based or temporary positions for specific durations
  • Independent Contractors: Specialized consultants for specific engagements
  • Internships and Learnerships: Opportunities for graduates and students to gain experience
  • Remote Work: Positions that can be performed remotely with appropriate arrangements

5. Offer and Onboarding

5.1 Job Offer

  • Successful candidates receive a written offer letter detailing terms and conditions of employment
  • Offers include information on salary, benefits, start date, probationary period, and reporting structure
  • Candidates typically have 5-7 business days to accept the offer
  • All offers are conditional upon satisfactory background checks and reference verification

5.2 Onboarding Process

  • New employees complete all required documentation before their start date
  • Comprehensive orientation program introduces company policies, culture, and systems
  • IT equipment, access credentials, and workspace are prepared in advance
  • New hires are assigned a mentor or buddy to support their integration
  • Regular check-ins during the probationary period ensure successful transition

6. Probationary Period

  • All new permanent employees are subject to a probationary period of 3-6 months
  • Performance is formally reviewed at the midpoint and end of the probationary period
  • Clear objectives and expectations are established at the beginning of employment
  • The probationary period may be extended by mutual agreement if additional evaluation time is needed
  • Either party may terminate employment during probation with reduced notice period as per employment contract

7. Internal Mobility

We encourage career development and internal mobility within Cronos IT Solutions:

  • Current employees are given preference for internal vacancies when qualifications are comparable
  • Employees must have been in their current role for at least 12 months before applying for internal positions
  • Employees must notify their current manager when applying for internal positions
  • Internal transfers require approval from both current and prospective managers
  • We support career progression through training, mentorship, and development opportunities

8. Referral Program

We value employee referrals and maintain a referral program to attract quality candidates:

  • Employees may refer qualified candidates for open positions
  • Referral bonuses are provided for successful hires who complete their probationary period
  • Referral bonus amounts vary by position level and are outlined in the referral program guidelines
  • Employees involved in hiring decisions cannot refer candidates for positions they are evaluating

9. Confidentiality and Data Protection

  • All candidate information is treated as confidential and processed in accordance with POPIA
  • Applicant data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel
  • Unsuccessful candidate information is retained for 12 months for future opportunities
  • Candidates may request deletion of their information at any time
  • Interview panel members sign confidentiality agreements regarding candidate information

10. Nepotism and Conflicts of Interest

  • Relatives of current employees may be hired based on merit and qualifications
  • Relatives cannot be in direct reporting relationships or positions where one supervises the other
  • Employees must disclose any family relationships with candidates during the recruitment process
  • Related employees working in the same department must be approved by senior management

11. Compliance and Review

  • This policy complies with South African labor laws, including the Employment Equity Act, Labour Relations Act, and Basic Conditions of Employment Act
  • Our hiring practices are regularly reviewed to ensure compliance and effectiveness
  • HR maintains records of all recruitment activities for audit and compliance purposes
  • This policy is reviewed annually and updated as needed to reflect legal or operational changes

We are committed to maintaining fair, transparent, and effective hiring practices that support our business goals and create opportunities for talented professionals.
