Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our consulting services, engagement models, and how we work with clients.

How does the consulting engagement process work? +
We begin every engagement with a discovery call to understand your business goals, technical challenges, and specific needs. For Strategic Advisory clients, we provide project-based consulting on an hourly basis with clear scope and deliverables outlined upfront. Consulting Retainer and Enterprise clients start with a comprehensive assessment period (typically 2-3 weeks) where we document your current IT environment, interview key stakeholders, and develop an initial strategic roadmap. We then establish regular meeting cadences, reporting structures, and communication protocols. All consulting deliverables—including assessments, recommendations, roadmaps, and documentation—are provided in professional formats suitable for executive review and decision-making.
Can I switch between consulting tiers or adjust my retainer hours? +
Yes, we offer flexibility to scale consulting services as your needs evolve. Strategic Advisory clients can upgrade to a Consulting Retainer at any time to gain dedicated monthly hours and priority support. Retainer clients can adjust their monthly hour allocation with 30 days' notice—we commonly customize retainers anywhere from 10 to 40+ hours monthly. Consulting Retainer plans require a 3-month minimum commitment, while Enterprise agreements are customized based on scope and typically include 12-month terms. If you're unsure which tier fits best, we're happy to discuss your specific situation and recommend the most cost-effective approach. Many clients start with Strategic Advisory for a specific project, then transition to a retainer once they see the value of ongoing strategic guidance.
What happens to unused retainer hours at the end of the month? +
Our Consulting Retainer and Enterprise plans include a monthly hour allocation. Unused hours do not roll over month-to-month, which encourages consistent engagement and ensures you're maximizing the value of your consulting partnership. However, we track your utilization patterns and proactively recommend adjustments if we notice you're consistently under or over your monthly allocation. If you need additional hours beyond your retainer in a given month, they're available at a reduced rate ($125/hour for Retainer clients). We provide monthly utilization reports so you always have visibility into how your consulting hours are being used. Most clients find that the retainer structure provides excellent value while maintaining budget predictability—typically 20-30% savings versus hourly-only consulting.
What types of organizations do you typically consult with? +
We work with organizations ranging from fast-growing startups to established enterprises across various industries. Our consultants have deep expertise in highly regulated sectors including healthcare (HIPAA), financial services (PCI-DSS, SEC), legal firms (attorney-client privilege protection), and professional services. Common client profiles include: companies planning significant IT investments or transformations, organizations navigating compliance requirements, businesses preparing for rapid growth or M&A activity, and leadership teams who need strategic technology guidance but don't require a full-time CTO. We're particularly effective for companies at inflection points—whether that's scaling from 50 to 200 employees, modernizing legacy systems, or entering new markets that require technology enablement. Our vendor-agnostic approach means we recommend solutions based on your specific needs, not commission relationships.
Do you implement the solutions you recommend, or just provide advice? +
Our IT Consulting services focus on strategic advisory, planning, and guidance—we help you make informed decisions and develop comprehensive roadmaps. We do not provide hands-on implementation, managed services, or helpdesk support (those are separate service offerings). Think of us as your strategic technology partner who helps you: evaluate options, design solutions, select vendors, plan projects, mitigate risks, and optimize costs. We work closely with your internal IT team or your chosen implementation partners to ensure successful execution of our recommendations. Many clients engage us for the strategic planning phase, then either handle implementation internally or work with implementation specialists we can recommend. This separation ensures you receive objective, vendor-agnostic advice focused solely on what's best for your business, not what we can implement or resell.
What are typical consulting projects or engagements? +
Common consulting engagements include: technology roadmap development (3-5 year strategic plans), cybersecurity assessments and strategy development, cloud migration planning and vendor selection, infrastructure architecture design, compliance gap analysis and remediation planning (HIPAA, SOC 2, GDPR), disaster recovery and business continuity planning, IT budget planning and cost optimization, vendor evaluation and contract negotiation support, M&A technology due diligence, and digital transformation strategy. Project timelines vary—a focused security assessment might take 2-3 weeks, while a comprehensive enterprise architecture review could span 2-3 months. We always provide a clear scope, timeline, and deliverables outline before beginning work. Most Strategic Advisory projects range from 10-40 hours total, while retainer clients typically use their hours for a combination of ongoing strategic guidance and focused project work throughout the year.
What qualifications and experience do your consultants have? +
Our consulting team consists of senior technology professionals with 15+ years of hands-on experience in IT leadership roles. Many of our consultants have served as CTOs, IT Directors, or Senior Architects for companies ranging from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. We maintain industry certifications including CISSP (security), TOGAF (enterprise architecture), AWS/Azure solutions architect certifications, and PMP (project management). More importantly, our consultants bring real-world experience having personally navigated the challenges you're facing—whether that's scaling infrastructure for rapid growth, managing compliance audits, negotiating enterprise software contracts, or leading digital transformation initiatives. We assign consultants based on industry expertise and technical specialization relevant to your needs. For Enterprise clients, you'll work with a dedicated lead consultant supported by specialists in areas like security, cloud architecture, or compliance as needed.
Do you work with remote and distributed teams? +
Yes, we work with clients across North America and internationally, and our consulting is delivered entirely remotely unless on-site presence is specifically requested (available for Enterprise clients). Remote consulting actually offers advantages—faster response times, flexible scheduling, reduced costs, and access to our full team of specialists regardless of geography. We conduct strategic planning sessions via video conference, share documentation through secure collaboration platforms, and maintain regular communication through your preferred channels (email, phone, Slack, Teams). Many of our consulting projects specifically focus on helping organizations optimize their technology for remote and hybrid work environments—including cloud infrastructure design, collaboration tool selection, security architecture for distributed teams, and remote workforce enablement strategies. Our consultants are experienced in working asynchronously across time zones and can accommodate your team's schedule.

Important Information

Engagement Terms: Strategic Advisory services are project-based with no minimum commitment. Consulting Retainer plans require a 3-month minimum commitment. Enterprise agreements are customized with typical 12-month terms and can include flexible scopes of work.

Consulting Hours: Retainer hours are allocated monthly and do not roll over. Time is tracked in 15-minute increments. Strategic Advisory and overage hours are billed at the end of each month. We provide detailed time tracking and monthly utilization reports for all retainer clients.

Scope of Services: Our consulting services provide strategic advisory, planning, and guidance. We do not provide hands-on implementation, managed IT services, helpdesk support, or 24/7 monitoring. Implementation of our recommendations can be handled by your internal team or implementation partners (we can provide referrals if needed).

Payment Terms: Monthly retainers are billed in advance on the 1st of each month. Strategic Advisory hours and retainer overages are invoiced at month-end. Net 15 payment terms apply to all services. We accept major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire payments.

Confidentiality: All consulting engagements include standard NDA coverage. We maintain strict confidentiality of your business information, technical architecture, and strategic plans. We can accommodate custom NDA requirements for Enterprise clients.
