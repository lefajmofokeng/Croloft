What is on-chain staking?

On-chain Staking is a great way for you to passively generate rewards from your cryptocurrency holdings, which might otherwise be sitting idle in your Crypto wallet.

When you stake your cryptocurrency on a blockchain protocol, you are participating in maintaining the protocol's security and are incentivized to do so by receiving rewards from the protocol in the form of staking yields.

Benefits of staking your crypto on-chain +

Staking provides network security, earns passive income, and is generally more environmentally friendly than traditional Proof-of-Work mining. It allows you to contribute directly to the decentralized governance and operation of the protocol.

How staking works +

In a Proof-of-Stake system, validators lock up (stake) a certain amount of the cryptocurrency as collateral. The protocol then randomly selects validators to propose and attest to new blocks, rewarding them with new coins or transaction fees for their honest work.

Considerations when staking crypto on-chain +

Key considerations include the 'lock-up' period (the time your funds are inaccessible), the risk of 'slashing' (losing staked funds due to validator misconduct), and the liquidity of your staked asset. Always research the protocol's reputation and underlying security mechanisms.

Is on-chain staking for you? +

Staking is ideal for long-term holders comfortable with the volatility of crypto assets and willing to commit their holdings for periods of time to earn rewards. If you require instant access to your funds, liquid staking solutions or other investment methods might be preferable.
