Frequently Asked Questions

When do I get paid?

Payouts are processed on the 15th of every month for commissions earned in the previous month.

Is there a limit on how much I can earn?

No. Our commissions are uncapped. The more business you bring, the more you earn.

Can I promote via paid ads?

Yes, as long as you don't bid on "Croloft" branded keywords or represent yourself as the company.
