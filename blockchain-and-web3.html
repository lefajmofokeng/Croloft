On-Chain Staking Solutions

Maximize returns on your digital assets with our secure, flexible, and convenient on-chain staking solutions.

Flexible

You can unstake your assets at any time once they are activated, providing liquidity when you need it.

Secure

We maintain separate blockchain addresses and wallets to facilitate the on-chain staking of your assets with maximum security.

Convenient

Put your idle assets to work in a few simple steps, and enjoy proportionate returns via regular payouts.

Please note that supported blockchains may independently impose minimum staking or unbonding periods. You will stop receiving any rewards during any unbonding period imposed by the protocol if you choose to unstake your assets. Rewards are proportionate to the amount staked and are determined by the blockchain protocol.

What blockchain platforms do you support?

+

We support all major blockchain platforms including Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain (BSC), Polygon, Solana, Avalanche, and other EVM-compatible chains. Our team has extensive experience with both public and private blockchain networks.

We can also help you choose the right blockchain platform based on your specific use case, scalability requirements, transaction costs, and business objectives.

How long does it take to develop a blockchain solution?

+

Development timelines vary based on project complexity:

  • Smart Contracts: 2-4 weeks for standard contracts
  • DApps: 8-16 weeks for full-stack applications
  • NFT Marketplaces: 12-20 weeks for complete platforms
  • Custom Solutions: Timeline determined after requirements analysis

We follow an agile development methodology with regular milestones and deliverables throughout the project.

What is included in your security audit service?

+

Our comprehensive security audit includes:

  • Manual code review by experienced blockchain security experts
  • Automated vulnerability scanning using industry-standard tools
  • Gas optimization analysis to reduce transaction costs
  • Testing against common attack vectors (reentrancy, overflow, etc.)
  • Detailed audit report with findings and severity ratings
  • Specific recommendations for fixes and improvements
  • Re-audit after implementing fixes to ensure security

Do you provide ongoing support after deployment?

Yes! All our solutions include post-deployment support ranging from 30 to 120 days depending on the package. Support includes:

  • Bug fixes and technical troubleshooting
  • Performance monitoring and optimization
  • Documentation and knowledge transfer
  • Minor updates and modifications

We also offer extended maintenance contracts for long-term support, continuous monitoring, and feature enhancements.

What are the costs associated with blockchain development?

Costs vary based on project scope and complexity. Our pricing structure includes:

What makes your blockchain solutions secure?

+

Security is our top priority. We implement multiple layers of protection:
