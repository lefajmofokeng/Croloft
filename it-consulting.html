Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our consulting process, deliverables, and expertise.

How do you deliver your consulting reports and roadmaps? +
We believe in actionable deliverables, not just talk. For Advisory sessions, we provide PDF summaries and high-level action items following our video calls. For Virtual CTO and Project-Based clients, we deliver comprehensive, professionally formatted architecture blueprints, cybersecurity frameworks, and multi-year IT roadmaps. We present these findings via executive workshops to ensure your entire leadership team is aligned on the technical direction.
Do you actually manage our IT or helpdesk tickets? +
No, we are strictly an IT consulting and advisory firm. We do not provide day-to-day managed IT services, helpdesk support, or automated patching. Our focus is purely on high-level strategy, architecture design, and executive leadership. If you require day-to-day operational support, we can help you vet, select, and negotiate with a Managed Service Provider (MSP) as part of our vendor selection services.
We don't have an internal backend team yet. Can you help? +
Absolutely. Many of the startups we partner with lack dedicated in-house backend engineers initially. We act as your technical sounding board, designing scalable architecture blueprints that you can hand off to freelance developers or future hires. When you're ready to build your internal team, our Virtual CTO service includes sitting in on technical interviews to ensure you hire the right talent for the tech stack we've helped you design.
What happens if I exceed my consulting hours on the Advisory plan? +
Advisory services are billed hourly at $150/hour with no predetermined limit. We track consultation and research time in 15-minute increments and always provide estimates before starting deep-dive research or drafting complex documents. If you find yourself consistently needing more than 8 hours of guidance a month, we will recommend transitioning to the Virtual CTO tier for better value and more structured roadmapping.
Are software licenses or cloud hosting included? +
No, our pricing covers professional strategic consulting only. We do not resell software or hardware. We are completely vendor-neutral, meaning our recommendations for AWS, Azure, Microsoft 365, or security tools are based entirely on what is best for your business, not on reseller commissions. We will help you optimize your budgets and negotiate directly with these vendors to save you money.
What compliance frameworks do you consult on? +
We provide strategic guidance and gap analysis for major regulatory frameworks including HIPAA, GDPR, and SOC 2 Type II. While we do not act as the final certifying auditor, our pre-audit preparation services ensure your technical architecture, access controls, and disaster recovery policies are structured correctly so you can pass your formal audits with confidence.

Important Information

Commitment Terms: Virtual CTO plans are billed monthly with a standard 30-day cancellation notice. Advisory services have no commitment. Project-Based consulting relies on custom Statements of Work (SOW).

Service Scope: All plans are purely consultative. Implementation of the provided strategies remains the responsibility of your internal staff, outsourced developers, or managed service providers.

Payment Terms: Monthly retainer plans are billed in advance. Advisory hours are invoiced at month-end. Net 15 payment terms for all services.
