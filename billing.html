Governance & Engagement Principles

1. Capacity Planning Limitations: Monthly resource structures guarantee baseline engineering focus. Drastic expansions or emergency product additions out-of-scope from current hour pools require temporary ad-hoc sprint extensions to preserve delivery cadences.

2. Hour Ledger Verification: Transparent, itemized timesheet ledgers detailing real-time tracking points are matched against active GitHub repositories and delivered during calendar transition phases.

3. Annual Termination Clauses: Annual agreements secure premium optimized rates. Early structural terminations trigger standard baseline structural recalculations back to rolling monthly costs for verified hours used.

4. Resource Allocation SOW: All custom server modifications, heavy structural code changes, and third-party API engine configurations operate strictly under signed master service statements before deployment to infrastructure.
