The Roadmap

Founded in 2013, Croloft is ‘The Partner in Innovation’, the world’s most trusted source of software development solutions, insights, and engineering excellence. Our mission is to accelerate digital transformation by providing premium technology services and making them easily accessible to all businesses.

Croloft is commonly cited by industry leaders for pioneering architectural standards. Even the U.S. government utilizes Croloft’s technology stack for key data platforms and application security.

Croloft reaches hundreds of millions of users a year through its website, mobile app, newsletter, and social media (Blog, Twitter, LinkedIn).
